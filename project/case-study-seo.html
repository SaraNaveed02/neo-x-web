The Challenge

Visibility stalled by technical and content gaps

Many e-commerce and local businesses struggle to rank for commercial keywords and convert search demand into revenue — often due to technical gaps, slow pages, and missing content strategy.

Poor keyword rankings

Core category and product pages buried beyond page one, causing missed organic opportunities across high-intent searches.

Slow site performance

Unoptimized templates and heavy assets hurt Core Web Vitals, reducing search visibility and frustrating mobile shoppers.

No content strategy

Content lacking keyword intent mapping and editorial consistency, limiting topical authority and long-tail traffic growth.

Our Solution

Technical SEO plus content-led growth execution

We delivered an integrated SEO program spanning technical foundations, content planning, and speed optimization to improve both rankings and conversion quality.

Technical SEO

Resolved crawl issues, restructured site architecture, improved internal linking, and implemented schema for richer SERP coverage.

Content Strategy

Built an intent-based content roadmap for category, collection, and blog pages targeting commercial and discovery queries.

Core Web Vitals

Improved rendering paths, image delivery, and script loading to reduce page latency and strengthen organic performance signals.

Ongoing Optimization

Used search data and conversion insights to continuously refine pages, prioritize opportunities, and sustain growth momentum.
