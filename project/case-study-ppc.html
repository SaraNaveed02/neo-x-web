The Challenge

Escalating costs and inconsistent lead quality

Many lead-generation businesses see rising acquisition costs and inconsistent lead quality when campaigns lack tight targeting, landing page alignment, and reliable tracking.

High CPA pressure

Acquisition costs rise month over month when budget is spread across broad, low-intent audiences.

Low-quality leads

Broad targeting and weak pre-qualification result in low-intent form fills with limited close potential.

Conversion journey gaps

Landing pages lacking message match and analytics clarity make it hard to optimize budget toward high-performing paths.

Our Solution

Precision targeting and conversion-first funnel optimization

We rebuilt the paid acquisition engine with tighter audiences, tailored landing experiences, and reliable tracking across every major touchpoint.

Audience Targeting

Segmented prospect pools by region, household profile, and purchase intent to improve lead relevance and budget efficiency.

Landing Page Optimization

Built dedicated conversion paths with better message match, simplified forms, and stronger proof points for qualified actions.

Conversion Tracking

Implemented GA4 and platform-level event tracking to measure lead quality, channel contribution, and true campaign ROI.

Weekly Optimization Cycles

Continuously refined creative, bidding strategy, and funnel stages using data from both ad platforms and on-site behavior.
