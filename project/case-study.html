The Challenge

A legacy platform holding back global growth

Legacy monoliths often cannot support modern UX expectations, mobile-first users, or traffic spikes — creating friction that hurts conversions and slows product delivery.

Performance bottlenecks

Slow page loads and failing Core Web Vitals directly impact SEO rankings and paid campaign efficiency.

Fragmented user experience

Inconsistent UI patterns, broken onboarding flows, and disconnected payment journeys increase drop-off at checkout.

Scaling limitations

Peak traffic periods cause downtime and manual workarounds — blocking the product team from shipping features at market speed.

Our Solution

Strategy, design, and engineering — end to end

NEOXWEB deployed a cross-functional squad combining product strategy, UX research, full-stack engineering, and DevOps to deliver a cloud-native platform built for scale.

Discovery & Audit

Technical debt mapping, user journey analysis, and competitive benchmarking to define a prioritized modernization roadmap.

UX & Design System

Conversion-focused UI redesign with a scalable component library — ensuring consistency across web, tablet, and mobile.

Cloud-Native Build

Microservices architecture on AWS with CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, and zero-downtime migration from the legacy monolith.

Launch & Optimize

Phased rollout with A/B testing, analytics instrumentation, and continuous performance tuning post-launch.
